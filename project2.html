+ Education

The Balkans Youth Council in cooperation with the most prestigious schools in the Republic of Albania such as: Tirana International School, the World Academy of Tirana and the Memorial International School of Tirana announce the newest project: + Education: The Art of Teaching

The project consists of a chain of training by experts and professionals of these schools, where they will train teachers on how to behave and operate with students in certain situations.

Based on our vision for education, the Balkans Youth Council is developing an international strategy for improving education in Albania and the Western Balkan countries. Our vision for this strategy is to support the key people or tools that are experts in promoting the principles of education quality. Our support to education brings social, political, cultural and economic development to our region.

The first training will be held on March 3, 2018, at the Xheko Imperial Hotel (Former Carlsberg) from 10:00 to 16:00. The lecturers who will lead the training will be as follows:

1- Robert S. Jackson – Director of Tirana International School

2- Birol Inaltekin – Director of Educational Services at Turgut Ozal Foundation

3- Suzanne Tomlinson – Academic Director of WAT (World Academy of Tirana)

4- Suhida Dermani – Project Coordinator at the Balkans Youth Council

The participation fee is 2,000 ALL and is open to all teachers and those who aspire to work in the education sector. Applications will be open until 28 February 2018. The training will be held in English.

For any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us at plus-edukim@bycouncil.org
